Privacy Policy
Effective: March 20, 2026 Last Updated: March 20, 2026
Dodge.fit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Dodge.fit mobile application. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.
1
About the App

Dodge.fit is a fitness management platform serving two types of users:

Member / User
  • Browse and register for fitness classes
  • Mark attendance via QR/barcode scan
  • Track class history and membership
  • Make payments for classes
Gym / Club Owner
  • Create and manage fitness classes
  • Manage member registrations
  • Generate QR/barcodes for attendance
  • View analytics and reports
2
Information We Collect

For Members / Users:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (registration)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Fitness class registrations and attendance records
  • Payment transaction history (processed via Razorpay)
  • QR/barcode scan data for attendance verification
  • Device information and app usage data

For Gym / Club Owners:

  • Business name, email address, and phone number
  • Gym/club location and details
  • Class schedules and member management data
  • Payment and revenue information (via Razorpay)
  • Device information and app usage data

Automatically Collected:

  • Device type, OS version, and unique device identifiers
  • App usage patterns and feature interaction data
  • Crash reports and performance data
3
App Permissions

Dodge.fit requests the following device permissions:

Permission Purpose Type
Camera Scanning QR codes and barcodes for attendance verification. Also used for uploading profile photos. Required
Storage / Media Saving and uploading profile photos from your device gallery. Optional
Internet Core app functionality — syncing data, processing payments, loading class information. Required
Notifications Sending class reminders, booking confirmations, and important updates. Optional
Camera Usage: The camera is used exclusively for scanning QR codes/barcodes to verify gym attendance and for optional profile photo uploads. We do not record video, take photos without your action, or use the camera for any other purpose.
4
How We Use Your Information
  • To create and manage your account and profile
  • To process class registrations and attendance tracking
  • To process payments securely through Razorpay
  • To send class reminders, booking confirmations, and notifications
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To improve app performance, features, and user experience
  • To generate attendance and revenue reports for gym owners
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
5
Payment Processing – Razorpay

All payment transactions within Dodge.fit are processed by Razorpay Software Private Limited, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.

We do not collect, store, or process your payment card numbers, CVV, or bank account details directly. All payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay and is governed by Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
6
Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Razorpay (payment processing) and other trusted vendors necessary to operate our services
  • Gym/Club Owners: Members' attendance and registration data is visible to the gym/club owner whose classes they have joined
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with prior notice to users
